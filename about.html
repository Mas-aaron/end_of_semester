About Francis Academy

Empowering learners worldwide through innovative education

OUR JOURNEY

From Humble Beginnings to Global Impact

Founded in 2015 by Francis Muganga, Francis Academy began as a small initiative to provide quality education to underserved communities in Uganda. What started as a single classroom with 20 students has grown into a global online learning platform serving over 250,000 learners across 35 countries.

Our founder's vision was simple yet powerful: to create an educational institution that breaks down barriers to learning, regardless of geographical location, economic status, or background. Today, we continue to uphold this vision while embracing technological advancements to make learning more accessible, engaging, and effective.

OUR PURPOSE

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To democratize education by providing affordable, high-quality learning experiences that empower individuals to achieve their personal and professional goals, regardless of their background or location.

Our Vision

To become the world's most accessible and impactful learning platform, transforming lives through education and creating opportunities for millions of learners across the globe.
